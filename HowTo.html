Piano Pro Guide

Hello and Welcome to Piano Pro! Piano Pro is an app intended for assisting both Sighted and Blind peoples better learn the Piano.

While most of the app is functional using your computer keyboard, it is possible to use piano pro with your electric piano! Follow this guide for Yamahas or Google your piano's manual for driver installation and connectivity.
